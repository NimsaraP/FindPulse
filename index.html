About FindPulse

The heartbeat of your digital fix

Our Story

FindPulse is more than just a tech platform—it's the heartbeat of your digital fix. Designed for Sri Lanka's fast-moving tech landscape, we seamlessly blend smart search, real-time support, and verified service listings into one sleek experience.

Our Mission

We believe that tech breakdowns are frustrating, but finding the right fix shouldn't be. Our mission is to eliminate endless Googling for repair shops, guessing which parts fit your device, and delays in diagnostics or service quotes.

What We Offer

Smart Technology

Our intelligent search and matching algorithms ensure you find exactly what you need, when you need it.

Verified Network

All service providers and parts suppliers are thoroughly vetted to ensure quality and reliability.

Local Focus

Built specifically for Sri Lankan users with local insights, pricing, and service networks.

Future Ready

Scalable platform ready to expand to groceries, vehicle repairs, bookshops, and any service you need.

Our Vision

With our clean interface, intelligent filters, and local-first approach, FindPulse empowers users to discover, diagnose, and solve tech issues faster than ever. We're building the future of service discovery—fast, reliable, and right at your fingertips.
